Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee brewed by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely-ground coffee beans. It features a bold, intense flavor with a thick, syrupy body and a layer of rich, golden crema on top. Typically served in small 1-ounce shots, espresso forms the foundation of many popular coffee drinks such as cappuccinos, lattes, and macchiatos. Its deep aroma, slight bitterness, and complex flavor notes make it a favorite among coffee aficionados.
